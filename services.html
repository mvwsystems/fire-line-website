What We Do

Underground
Utility Services

Full-scope commercial underground utility installation and repair across the DFW metroplex and surrounding regions. One sub. All four lines.

01
Core Service

Sewer Installation

We install commercial gravity and pressure sewer systems for projects of all sizes — from tilt-wall warehouses and multi-family complexes to hospitals, hotels, and roadway infrastructure. Every install is built to local code and coordinated with the GC's schedule.

Our crews are experienced with tight utility corridors, congested job sites, and fast-track schedules. We get in, get it done, and get out.

  • Gravity sewer mains and laterals
  • Pressure sewer force mains
  • Lift station coordination
  • New construction & retrofit installs
  • Commercial & institutional projects
02
Core Service

Water Line Install

Domestic and commercial water service installations for commercial pad sites, multi-family developments, and institutional campuses. We work with the jurisdiction and engineer to make sure every install is done right the first time.

  • Domestic water service mains
  • Fire service water feeds
  • Meter set and connection work
  • New construction & replacement
03
Core Service

Storm Drainage

Underground storm drainage systems engineered for high-volume commercial and infrastructure projects. From large-diameter RCP to detention tie-ins, we handle the full storm scope on complex commercial sites.

  • Storm sewer mains and inlets
  • Large-diameter RCP installations
  • Detention pond outfall structures
  • Roadway and infrastructure drainage
04
Core Service — Where It All Started

Fire Line Install

Underground fire protection line installation is in our DNA. Since 1992, we've been installing fire lines and FDC connections for overhead sprinkler subs across the DFW metroplex. It's where Fire Line Services began — and still one of our strongest capabilities.

  • Underground fire mains
  • FDC (Fire Department Connection) piping
  • Backflow preventer vaults
  • Fire hydrant leads and laterals
  • Post indicator valve (PIV) installations
